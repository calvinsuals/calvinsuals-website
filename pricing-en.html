Photography Guidelines

For Booking Process

  • To book a photoshoot, a 50% deposit is required. The shoot date will be confirmed once the deposit is received. The deposit is valid for 180 days.

For Delivery Time

  • The photo delivery time will begin after the balance payment is received. Under normal circumstances, the delivery time for a set of photos is 14 days, excluding public holidays. (We offer expedited delivery: $30 for delivery within 7 days, $50 for delivery within 3 days, depending on the number of photos.) The final edited photos will be provided to you (automotive and motorcycle photos do not include raw files; additional fees will apply if raw files are required).

For Photo Details

  • If you require additional edited photos or raw images, please inquire. Photos will be deleted within 24 hours after the final images are delivered, and no copies will be kept.

Other Terms

  • Cancellations and No-Shows: If you cancel the booking or fail to complete the shoot, the entire deposit will be forfeited as a penalty and will not be refunded.
  • Rescheduling: One reschedule is allowed without penalty. However, rescheduling within 48 hours will not be accepted. Each reschedule will incur a 50% penalty fee from the deposit. If the full deposit is not paid within 90 days, the booking will be automatically canceled, and the deposit will be forfeited.
  • Weather Conditions: In case of extreme weather conditions, we may allow rescheduling without penalty, but this will be subject to approval based on the situation.
  • Liability: The photographer is not responsible for any damages or accidents occurring during the photoshoot, including but not limited to property damage or personal injury.
  • Use of Photos on Social Media: If you do not wish for the photos to be posted on social media, an additional fee of $30 will apply.
  • Intellectual Property: All images created during the photoshoot are the property of the photographer. The client is granted a license to use the images for personal purposes, but any commercial use must be approved in advance and may require additional fees.
Please ensure that you have read all terms. In case of any dispute, calvinsuals reserves the right of final interpretation.
